🏊 Irrawaddy Invitationals 2026

Dates: 31 July - 1 August 2026

Venue: StarCity Sports Complex, Myanmar

Creating opportunities for both swimmers and dolphins across Myanmar.

About the Event

The Irrawaddy Invitationals aims to provide swimmers throughout Myanmar with opportunities to participate in a professionally organised competition environment while supporting dolphin conservation efforts.
